Origin Story

My name is Todd Vorasanaga. I grew up in Hawaii and I love cooking and sharing my food to all of my family and friends. Living in Hawaii and expiriencing alot of different type of cuisine, I noticed a lack for my country's cousine, Thai cuisine. My Family and friends pushed me to first open our first store in Honolulu, HI. With the booming demand of our cuisine, we branched out to the Mainland California with 3 more Locations.
